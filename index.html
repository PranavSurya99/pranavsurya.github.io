About Me

I am a public policy researcher trained at the Indian Institute of Technology, Tirupati, with strong academic grounding in economics and applied policy analysis. My interests focus on evaluating public programs, fiscal systems, and institutional design, particularly in contexts affecting vulnerable populations.

I have applied research experience at the Institute for Social and Economic Change (ISEC), Bengaluru, where I worked on large-scale climate advisory services under the Varuna Mitra program. My work involved field research, stakeholder engagement, institutional diagnostics, and monitoring framework development.

Beyond academics, I have served as Placement Representative for the MPP program at IIT Tirupati. I am a state-level yoga medalist and actively engage in policy discussions and debates.
